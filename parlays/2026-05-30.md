### RECOMMENDED TICKET — 2 legs, +209

Goal = **best realistic chance of winning at ~+200**. On a coin-flip-heavy slate
with no fairly-priced heavy favorite, the highest-floor path is **two moderate
favorites (~-130) in separate games, each backed by a real pitching/offense edge**,
rather than one steep anchor + filler. Two ~58-59% legs is the ceiling for a +200
target (which carries a ~32% breakeven by definition).

| Leg | Bet | Price | Decimal | True est. |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **San Diego Padres ML** (@ WSH) | -130 | 1.769 | ~59% |
| 2 | **Cleveland Guardians ML** (vs BOS) | -134 | 1.746 | ~56% |

  edge **~+0.6pp**. Honestly a *thin* edge — this is a "best-available-chance"
  ticket, not a value smash. Framed as ~one-in-three to cash.
